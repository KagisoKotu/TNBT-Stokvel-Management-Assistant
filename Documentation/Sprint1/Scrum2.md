## Meet up with Client

The team agreed to schedule a meeting with the client (tutor) on the following day to clarify the requirements for Sprint 1 as outlined in the sprint documentation. In preparation, the team identified the following key questions to raise:

- Which technology stack is recommended for the project?
- Which tools are recommended for tracking sprint and project progress (e.g., Trello, Taiga, or GitHub Actions)?

## Choose Specifications

The team reviewed the Sprint 1 documentation in detail to ensure a thorough understanding of the sprint requirements, including:

- Expected deliverables
- Assigned tasks
- Overall project objectives

**Role Allocations:**

| Role | Responsibilities |
|------|------------------|
| Secretary | Maintaining all project documentation throughout the sprint, including recording and distributing meeting notes, documenting decisions, and tracking updates to all project-related documents |
| Scrum Master | Facilitating team meetings (including daily stand-ups), ensuring the team adheres to Agile principles, and keeping the team aligned with sprint goals and deadlines |
