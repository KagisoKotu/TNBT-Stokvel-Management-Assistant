## Meet up with Client

The team convened to finalise task allocation, agree on daily stand-up meeting arrangements, and discuss the UI design, user flow, and authentication approach for the Sprint 1 deliverables. The client was not present at this internal meeting.

**Daily Stand-Up Meetings:**

The team agreed to hold daily stand-up meetings to track progress, address blockers, and maintain alignment across all workstreams. These meetings will take place every day until Sunday, 12 April. The stand-ups will be attended by team members only and are intended to be brief and focused.

---

## Choose Specifications

**UI Design, User Flow, and Authentication:**

The team discussed the overall user interface design and agreed on the following specifications:

| Aspect | Specification |
|--------|---------------|
| Visual Design | Colour schemes, layout standards, and visual consistency must be maintained across all pages |
| User Journey | Full user journey mapped from Sign-Up page (User Story 1) through to Role-Based Redirection page |
| Page Components | For each page, the team identified the required components, user interactions, and expected functionality to be implemented |
| Authentication | Google Auth will be implemented in accordance with the recommended technology stack |

**Task Allocation and Work Structure:**

The project work for Sprint 1 was divided into two main components: frontend and backend. Each component was further broken down into three smaller tasks, resulting in a total of six tasks. Each task was assigned to a specific team member to ensure clear ownership, accountability, and parallel progress across the team.
