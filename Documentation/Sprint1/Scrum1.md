# Scrum 1

# Objectives

1. Review project brief independently
2. Justify and select team projects
3. Finalize ranked project list

---

## Meet up with Client

The team convened following the release of the project brief. Each member independently reviewed the brief, conducted additional research, and identified three suitable projects. The client was not present in these internal meetings, but the project brief provided the necessary specifications and constraints.

---

## Choose Specifications

The team held two internal meetings to filter and select projects based on feasibility, resources, and team preference.

Each team member was tasked with:

- Reviewing the project brief thoroughly
- Conducting additional research as needed
- Identifying and documenting three preferred projects


Each member presented their chosen projects with justifications. The team then voted to determine the three most preferred projects.

**Final Project Selection — Ranked Results**

| Rank | Project Name |
|------|----------------|
| 1 | Stokvel Management Platform |
| 2 | Campus Food Ordering Platform |
| 3 | Community Clinic Appointment and Queue Management System |
